What is a Peapod?

In a traditional crossword, each cell contains a single letter, and you read the fill cellwise left to right and top to bottom.

A peapod crossword (or just "peapod") is a crossword puzzle where each cell contains two letters. Otherwise, it's exactly like a traditional crossword!
